Cloudflare 

Dave September 7, 2022

Cloudflare for those that dont know is a SAAS based service that provides Web Applicaiton Firewall protection and DDOS protection. They are the world leader in protecting sites and they offer a free plan that can be used by creators. In addition to this they offer a product call Cloudfalre pages which is a static web page storage that is create through GitHub and then deployed through CI/CD

Cloudflare also offers DNS and Domain registration at cost pricing and makes managing domains simples and easy to do.
